**1.	Project Title / Headline**
Business Performance Dashboard using Power BI

**2.	Short Description / Purpose** :- 
Sales Performance KPIs
Provides a high-level overview of sales performance by tracking key business metrics such as total sales, order volume, customer coverage, and sales trends. It enables management to monitor overall business performance and identify areas requiring attention.

Activity Performance
Monitors the day-to-day activities of the sales team by analysing customer visits, productive calls, visit frequency, working hours, and activity completion. This report helps evaluate field execution and improve sales efficiency.

Field Force Productivity
Measures the productivity of field sales representatives through KPIs such as total visits, productive vs. non-productive visits, average working hours, customer coverage, and sales per representative. It helps managers assess employee performance and optimise field operations.

Customer Analytics
Provides insights into customer behaviour by analysing unique customers, repeat vs. new customers, inactive customers, customer classifications, and purchasing patterns. This report supports customer retention strategies and business growth.

**3.	Tech Stack**
•	📊 Power BI Desktop – Data visualisation and interactive dashboard development 
•	📈 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Custom measures, KPIs, calculated columns, and business logic 
•	🔄 Power Query (M Language) – Data extraction, cleaning, transformation, and shaping 
•	🗄️ Microsoft Excel – Source data preparation and validation 
•	🧩 Data Modelling – Relationships, star schema, and optimised data model 
•	📉 Data Visualisation – KPI Cards, Matrix, Bar Charts, Line Charts, Pie/Doughnut Charts, Slicers, and Drill-through 
•	⚡ Performance Optimisation – Efficient DAX measures and optimised report performance

**4.	Data Source**
•	Microsoft Excel (.xlsx) – Sales activity and customer visit data used as the primary data source. 
•	Data imported into Power BI using Power Query for cleaning and transformation.


**5.	Features / Highlights**
Business Problem
Sales managers and business leaders often struggle to monitor field sales activities and customer engagement due to scattered data across multiple Excel files and manual reporting processes. This makes it difficult to measure sales team productivity, identify inactive customers, track customer visits, and make timely, data-driven decisions.

The organisation needed a centralised and interactive dashboard to:
•	Monitor sales representatives' daily performance. 
•	Track customer visits and coverage. 
•	Identify new, repeat, and inactive customers. 
•	Analyse customer performance by class and type. 
•	Measure working hours and field productivity. 
•	Monitor monthly sales activity trends. 
•	Replace manual reporting with automated, interactive dashboards. 
•	Enable faster decision-making through real-time KPIs and visual analytics. 

Solution
Developed an interactive Power BI Sales Activity & Performance Dashboard that consolidates sales and customer visit data into a single platform. The dashboard provides real-time KPIs, customer analytics, productivity metrics, and trend analysis using Power BI, Power Query, and DAX, enabling stakeholders to monitor performance, improve operational efficiency, and make data-driven business decisions.

Goal of the dashboard
The primary goal of this dashboard is to provide a centralised, interactive view of sales activities and field force performance, enabling stakeholders to monitor key business metrics, evaluate customer engagement, and make informed, data-driven decisions.

Objectives
•	📊 Interactive and dynamic Power BI dashboard 
•	📈 Real-time KPI tracking for sales and field activities 
•	👥 Unique, New, Repeat, and Inactive Customer analysis 
•	🏪 Customer Class-wise and Customer Type-wise performance analysis 
•	📅 Month-wise sales and customer visit trends 
•	⏱️ Sales representative working hours and productivity tracking 
•	🎯 Productive vs. Non-Productive visit analysis 
•	📍 Customer coverage and visit frequency monitoring 
•	📌 Interactive slicers for Month, Sales Representative, Customer, and Region 
•	📉 Drill-down and cross-filtering for detailed analysis 
•	⚡ Optimized DAX measures for accurate KPI calculations 
•	🔄 Automated data transformation using Power Query 
•	📱 Clean, responsive, and business-friendly dashboard design
Walk Through of Key Visuals
1. KPI Cards
Displays high-level business metrics such as Total Visits, Unique Customers, Productive Visits, Inactive Customers, Average Order Value, and Total Working Hours, providing an instant overview of sales performance.
2. Monthly Trend Analysis
Line and column charts visualize month-wise sales activities, customer visits, and performance trends, helping identify seasonal patterns and growth opportunities.
3. Customer Analytics
Breaks down customers into New, Repeat, and Inactive categories, enabling the business to evaluate customer retention and acquisition performance.
4. Customer Class & Type Analysis
Compares performance across different customer classes and customer types, helping identify the most valuable customer segments.
5. Field Force Performance
Analyses sales representative productivity using metrics such as customer visits, productive calls, working hours, and customer coverage.
6. Productivity & Working Hours
Shows the total and average working hours of the sales team, helping managers monitor field efficiency and resource utilization.
7. Interactive Filters (Slicers)
Allows users to filter the dashboard by Month, Sales Representative, Customer, Customer Type, and other dimensions, enabling customized analysis.
8. Drill-Down & Cross-Filtering
Users can click on visuals to drill down into detailed information and automatically cross-filter related charts for deeper business insights.
